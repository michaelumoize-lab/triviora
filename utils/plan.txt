# 🔥 Design Tips (Important for your theme)

* Use **lots of spacing** → premium feel
* Keep colors **subtle (Tailwind defaults)**
* Use **rounded-xl / 2xl**
* Add **soft shadows (shadow-sm / shadow-md)**
* Avoid clutter — clarity wins
